RFNEXx-Lite: Streamline Your Warehouse Management

Overview - RFNEXx-Lite is a pre-defined warehouse template designed to simplify and accelerate your SAP EWM implementation. With a focus on basic warehouse processes, this solution provides a solid foundation for managing your inventory and order fulfillment.

Key Features -

  • Inbound Process: Pre-built RF transactions for efficient goods receipt, including prebuild material labels and putaway forms.
  • Internal Process: Pre-built RF transactions for physical inventory process, bin-to-bin transaction query, and more.
  • Outbound Process: Pre-built RF transactions for standard picking process, including printing pick lists and pack slips for outbound deliveries.
  • Documentation: Print warehouse order forms and physical inventory forms with ease.
